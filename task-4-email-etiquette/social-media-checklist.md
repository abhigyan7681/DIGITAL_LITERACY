#Do's
1) Do use privacy settings to control who sees your personal content.
2) Do proofread your posts for clarity and tone before hitting "send."
3) Do share your achievements, such as coding projects or certifications.
4) Do think about how a future employer would perceive your profile.
5) Do follow and engage with industry leaders in your field of study.

#Don't 
1) Don't post confidential information about yourself or others.
2) Don't engage in "flame wars" or public arguments with peers or faculty.
3) Don't post content that depicts illegal activities or policy violations.
4) Don't use unprofessional or offensive language, even in "private" groups.
5) Don't vent about work or school frustrations in a public forum.
