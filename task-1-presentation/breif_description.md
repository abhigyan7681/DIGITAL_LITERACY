# Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

This assignment focuses on the development of a single-page infographic that defines digital literacy and highlights its significance for today’s students. The content is organized into four key pillars: a foundational definition of digital literacy, essential digital tools, online safety protocols, and strategies for maintaining a professional digital footprint. Created using Canva, this visual guide aims to translate complex concepts into an accessible format that promotes responsible and effective technology use.
